Background

Having personally encountered precarious situations, I developed a strong appreciation for the important role played by first responders. They help you when you can’t help yourself, providing safety and security. I learned from a young age that I wanted to extend that sense of relief to others.

My journey began with the Big Boulder Ski Patrol's young adult patroller program. At 15, I dedicated the summer of 2019 to studying Outdoor Emergency Care (OEC), earning my certification for patrolling that same year. I volunteered at Big Boulder for two seasons until I began studying at Bard College. I remained in Massachusetts for two years, which prevented me from patrolling. Nevertheless, I used this time to expand my knowledge and experience by becoming a lifeguard and an Emergency Medical Responder. During my year at Dartmouth College, I connected with a local mountain in need of patrolling assistance. Now, I contribute to slope safety and enjoy engaging with the Lebanon skiing community.

Certifications:

  • Outdoor Emergency Care (OEC includes CPR & BLS)
  • Lifeguarding
  • Emergency Medical Responder (EMR)

Current location: Storrs Hill
